About Our Company

EM2 Machining has delivered precision-machined components in Greensboro, North Carolina, for nearly 20 years. Our small, skilled team specializes in aerospace and automatic machining, combining expertise with advanced equipment to handle complex manufacturing needs.

We take pride in our ability to reliably and efficiently produce high-tolerance parts. From prototyping to production runs, every part we machine is backed by careful planning, close inspection, and a commitment to quality. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification reflects our dedication to continuous improvement and customer satisfaction.

At EM2, we understand that strong relationships and clear communication are just as important as technical precision. Whether you're a large-scale manufacturer or a local business, we work with you directly to meet your deadlines and exceed your expectations.
